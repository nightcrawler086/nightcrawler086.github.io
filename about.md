## a little about me

I've been in the IT industry in various roles over the past 9 years.  My focuses have ranged from networking to virtualization to storage.  I currently work as a consultant for a well-known storage company.  

I've been meaning to have my own blog for a while, and am just now getting around it.  Let's call it a new year's resolution.  Another one of those resolutions, and the primary reason for this blog, is the shift in the way companies use and consume infrastructure.  Public clouds like AWS, Azure, and Google are becoming the first choice for infrastrcutre instead companies using their own datacenters.  For that reason, I mean to evolve from a product specialist into something a bit higher up the stack.
