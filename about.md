## a little about me

I've been in the IT industry in various roles over the past 10 years.  My focuses have ranged from networking to virtualization to storage.  I currently work as a consultant for a well-known storage company.  

I've been meaning to have my own blog for a while, and am just now getting around it.  Let's call it a new year's resolution.  The real reason for this blog is that I'm trying to transition from a more traditional infrastructure engineer to a next-generation infrastructure engineer (or a full-stack engineer).  My background has been in datacenter technology, which is where I want to stay, but my experience has been platform specific (EMC, Netapp, VMware, Cisco, etc).  I want to focus a little higher up the stack.  This blog will be about that journey.
